{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Apps Get the best previewing experience on mobile devices with apps designed especially for previewing prototypes with Framer. ---
Framer for Android

The offical Framer for Android app, with a full-screen chromeless browser and automatic detection of all open projects.

Full-screen browsing

A chromeless browser that hides all controls for an immersive experience.

Customizable gestures

Swipe up or shake to reveal the controls and swipe left and right between projects.

Connect with Studio

Instantly detects all your open projects, and those of others on your network.

---
Frameless for iOS

An iOS8 browser made for designers and developers who need an easy way to preview prototypes on iOS devices.

--- ##### Browse Prototypes To see all of your active documents within a web browser, simply remove your project name from the URL to get to the root of the server.
On iOS, you can add your project to your Homescreen by tapping the Share button and then "Add to Home Screen". This allows you to view your prototypes without browser chrome. ---
