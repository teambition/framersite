{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Previewing Apps Get the best previewing experience on mobile devices with apps designed especially for previewing prototypes with Framer. ---
Framer for Android

The offical Framer for Android app, with a full-screen chromeless browser and automatic detection of all open projects.

---
Frameless for iOS

An iOS8 browser made for designers and developers who need an easy way to preview prototypes on iOS devices.

--- ##### On Browser If you've sent yourself the link to your prototype via email or message, opening it on your mobile device automatically launches the browser with your prototype. And with the new **Handoff** feature in iOS8, opening the link on your Mac also grants you quick access on mobile. To see all of your active documents, simply remove your project name from the URL to get to the root of the server.
On iOS, you can add your project to your Homescreen by tapping the Share button and then "Add to Home Screen". This allows you to view your prototypes without browser chrome. ---
