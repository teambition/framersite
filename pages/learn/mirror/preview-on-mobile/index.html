{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Preview on Mobile To view your prototype on mobile, make sure your mobile device is on the same WiFi network as your Mac. If you've sent yourself the link to your prototype via email or message, opening it on your mobile device automatically launches the browser with your prototype. And with the new **Handoff** feature on iOS8, opening the link on your Mac also grants you quick access to the prototype on mobile. --- ##### Browse prototypes To see all of your active documents, simply remove your project name from the URL to get to the root of the server.

**On iOS**, you can add your project to your Homescreen by tapping the Share button and then **"Add to Home Screen"**. This allows you to view your prototypes without browser chrome. --- ##### Notes - The **inital-scale** meta tag is set to 0.5 by default, for creating prototypes for Retina devices.
- To save your prototype for offline viewing, add /offline.html to the URL. ---
